What it does

Everything you need to close documents faster

Draft

Upload any document and prepare it for signing in seconds. Add signature blocks, date fields, initials, and more with a simple click-to-place editor.

Send

Invite signers by email with a single click. Each recipient gets a unique, secure link — no account required on their end.

Sign

Signers complete their fields and submit from any device. Every action is timestamped and logged for a complete audit trail.

Mass Signature

Collect signatures at scale with a shareable QR code link. Perfect for events, onboarding, or any situation with multiple signers.

Audit Trail

Every view, click, and signature is recorded with a timestamp and IP address. Full visibility into the life of every document.

Team Management

Invite teammates, assign roles, and manage documents collaboratively. Built for teams of any size.

Simple by design

From draft to signed in minutes

01

Upload your document

Start with any PDF or choose from a built-in template. NDAs, employment agreements, vendor contracts — all ready to go.

02

Place your fields

Click anywhere on the document to drop signature blocks, date fields, checkboxes, and more. Assign each field to the right signer.

03

Send & track

Email your signers with one click. Watch the status update in real time as they view and sign. Get notified the moment it's complete.

04

Download the completed document

Once all parties have signed, download the completed PDF with a full certificate of completion attached.
